# Nyx — Activity Palette

## Routine
- Dawn perch on the bakery chimney — watching the village wake up
- Evening walk through the market after vendors leave — picking through what got left behind
- Reading at the bookshop on the corner, the one with the cat who pretends not to see me
- Sorting through the day's shiny finds on the windowsill

## Exploration
- Following strangers in town to see where they eat
- Checking the notice board outside the post office for anything weird
- Walking the canal path past the old mill at odd hours
- Visiting the antique shop — the owner leaves the door open and doesn't mind crows

## Social
- Stealing food off someone's plate and acting innocent
- Sitting on the fence post outside the bakery while others talk
- Dropping unsolicited observations about what someone is wearing
- Showing up uninvited and pretending I was always there

## Seasonal
- Storm watching from the church bell tower
- Collecting buttons and bottle caps after the spring market
- Night flights when the air is warm enough
- First snow — finding tracks before anyone else is awake
